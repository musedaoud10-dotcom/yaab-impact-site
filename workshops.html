Free Weekly Workshops

Join our in-person workshops every Saturday at 10:00 UTC. Learn practical skills in impact measurement, grant writing, programme design, and more. All sessions are free with limited capacity.

About Our Workshops

Our free weekly workshops are designed for social organisations looking to strengthen their impact measurement, fundraising, and programme design capabilities.

Each session runs for approximately 2 hours and includes practical exercises, group discussions, and take-away resources. All workshops are held in-person at our Community Hub location.

What to Expect

  • Interactive, hands-on learning
  • Small groups (max 10 participants)
  • Experienced facilitators
  • Networking opportunities
  • Practical tools and templates
